This label was created through a project of the Comics School based in Naples. The goal was to create a label for a medium-high quality wine from Benevento(a city of Campania, Italy) and develop three types of wine: Aglianico, Rosato and Bianco. My initial inspiration was base on the Calydonian boar, one of the main symbols of Benevento. I have chosen an accurate font and then I modified it adding wine bubbles and filling the inside of the letters with the color associated with the wine as if they were glasses. In the lower part of the label I added a spot of wine to represent the elegance of the wine in a more attractive way.
