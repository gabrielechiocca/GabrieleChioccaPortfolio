This logo was created through a project of the Comics School based in Naples. The aim was to develop a logo for a fast food completely created by us. First I did some researches to find inspiration and examples from various fast food, then I choose what targets and features my fast food should have. In a second moment, I dedicated myself to the choice of the name, by making it not too complex and above all to make the idea quickly. I started working on the design of the logo, making more and more sketches, to come to the conclusional choice. The name BunRush comes from the combination of two single words "Bun" and "Rush" which respectively mean bread (used for hot dogs) and fast, to make the idea of “fast food”.
