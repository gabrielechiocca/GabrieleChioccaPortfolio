SAY CIAO! ABOUT ME

Logo BunRush

This logo was created through a project of the Comics School based in Naples. The aim was to develop a logo for a fast food completely created by us. First I did some researches to find inspiration and examples from various fast food, then I choose what targets and features my fast food should have.

Calenno Wine

This label was created through a project of the Comics School based in Naples, the goal was to create a wine that reflects Benevento(a city of Campania, Italy) and its origins. My idea was to create an icon that reflects the Calydonian boar, one of the main symbols of Benevento and I added details for each different type of wine in order to distinguish them clearly.

Logo FlyMeet

The creation of this logo was born from a Start2Impact project, the goal was to develop a logo for an application designed by us. The aim of the application was to know people and travel with strangers from all over the world. I created the logo trying to make the message clear starting from the name FlyMeet which means traveling and meeting people.
